Developer

Get started with OSGi. Reuse components to build and manage your highly complex systems. Make code easier to write, test and reuse. Manage dynamic deployment. Detect bugs earlier. Deploy remotely. Detect and solve problems you might not be aware of right now. If you’re developing software in Java, then OSGi should be in your tool chest.

Business

Create new and incremental revenue with dynamic services and/or the cross-industry inclusion of multiple devices and applications. Save time and money on development, maintenance and remote service management.

enRoute

Get started with OSGi application development. Develop modular and dynamic applications with OSGi and benefit from the additional resources and functionalities provided by the OSGi enRoute application framework.
